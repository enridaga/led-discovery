<p>A composer conducting his own work usually looks ridiculous. There are a few exceptions, but Berg didn&rsquo;t add to the list. As soon as he started waving his arms, the wonderful Maryinsky Theatre orchestra disintegrated, each member pulling in his own direction. / It did not bode well, but the situation was saved by Vladimir Dranishnikov, the theatre&rsquo;s chief conductor. He stood behind Berg and signalled the orchestra, Berg didn&rsquo;t notice a thing because he was so engrossed in the process of conducting.</p>
