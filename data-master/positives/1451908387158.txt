<p>Leonhard Emil Bach has for some two years past&nbsp;been frequently and conspicuously before the London&nbsp;public, which has signified its delight in his admirable&nbsp;performances by ample tribute of the applause they&nbsp;have so richly merited...Under&nbsp;the hands of Leonhard Bach &mdash; who has profited largely by&nbsp;the instruction he was fortunate enough to receive from&nbsp;his illustrious master, Franz Liszt &mdash; the piano not only&nbsp;discourses most excellent music, but, at times, assumes&nbsp;an orchestral character.</p>
