<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;">The tenth day of August was the barber surgeons' feast. And they kept their communion at St. Alphage at Cripplegate. And Mr. Richardson did preach, the Scot. There was good&nbsp;singing, and after, to their hall to dinner. And after dinner, a play.</span></p>
