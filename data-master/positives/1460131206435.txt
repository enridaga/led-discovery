<p>Lyapunov had transcribed the Overture to <em>Ruslan and Lyudmila</em> for two pianos, and in the morning we rehearsed it together to play as the opening number, Lyapunov on piano one and I on piano two. During the applause I made him step forward to take a bow as the author, and the old man beamed as though he himself had been the composer of <em>Ruslan and Lyudmila</em>.</p>
