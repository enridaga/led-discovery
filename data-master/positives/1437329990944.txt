<pre>The prices at the theatre, on the nights Catalani performed, were raised to half <br />a guinea for the pit and boxes, and five shillings for the gallery. At the <br />piano-forte sat my old, revered, and first teacher, Dr. Cogan. Madame Catalan! <br />was received, and hailed by the Irish audience, with rapturous applause. She sang <br />divinely, both in the serious and comic operas. I always acted with her, and had <br />a hearty reception from my generous countrymen.</pre>
