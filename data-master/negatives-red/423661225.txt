"I was ... thankful for the monthly and quarterly magazines that lay on the tables of the library, for their instructive articles were equal to anything I could have heard in the lecture-room. From the theological entanglements of <i>Hibbert's Journal</i>, I passed to the more worldly regions of the <i>English Review</i>, on to the literary vastness of the <i>Edinburgh</i>, through the critical orbs of the <i>Criterion</i>, and completed the circle with many others that improved my knowledge of the -isms and -ologies that were constantly under the eye of speculation.'"
