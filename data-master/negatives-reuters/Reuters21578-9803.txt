West German wholesale turnover
provisionally totalled about 58 billion marks in February, a
rise of a real one pct compared with the same month last year,
the Federal Statistics Office said.
    Turnover fell a nominal seven pct from February last year,
down by around four billion marks, it added. Turnover of about
114 billion marks in the first two months of 1987 represented a
real decline of two pct from the same 1986 period.
    According to final figures, wholesale turnover had fallen
by a real 5.7 pct in January, an office spokeswoman said.
