Westport Bancorp Inc said it
declared a 100 pct stock dividend and increased the quarterly
cash dividend.
    The company said the the stock dividend, to effect a
two-for-one stock split, is payable July six to holders of
record June 12.
    It said the dividend on present shares was increased to 26
cts, from 25 cts in the prior quarter, payable July one to
holders of record June 12.
