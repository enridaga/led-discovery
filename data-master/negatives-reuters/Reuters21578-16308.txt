&lt;Southern Air Transport> said it sued
WPLG-Channel 10 of Miami for compensatory and punitive damages
in excess of 50 mln dlrs for false and defamatory statements
made by the television station in a series of broadcasts last
month.
    The complaint alleges that the broadcast contained numerous
false statements which the stations knew were untrue.
    Officials of either company were unavailable for comment.
