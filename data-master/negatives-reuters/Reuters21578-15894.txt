Fujitsu Ltd &lt;ITSU.T> has forecast that
group net profits will rise 75.9 pct in the current year to 38
billion yen and current profits will rise 72.6 pct to 65
billion, the company said in its earnings statement.
    Sales are expected to rise 14 pct to 2,040 billion yen in
the same period, it said.
