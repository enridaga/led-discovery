Wisconsin Energy Corp's Wisconsin
Electric Power Co said it will redeem its 100 dlr par value
serial preferred stock on April 14.
    Wisconsin Electric said it will redeem 333,325 shares of
its 8.9 pct preferred at 101 dlrs a share, 225,810 shares of
its 7.75 pct preferred at 101 dlrs a share, and 140,967 shares
of its 8.8 pct preferred at 105.87 dlrs a share.
    The redemption will be financed from a new issue of the
company's preferred stock.
    First Wisconsin Trust Co will be the redemption agent.
