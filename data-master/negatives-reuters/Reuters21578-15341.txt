Central bank governors
from Spain, Latin America and the Caribbean will meet here this
week at two separate conmferences to discuss finance and
monetary problems, the Central Bank of Barbados said.
    The 24th session of central bank governors of the american
continent opens today for two days. Central bank governors of
Latin America and Spain will hold a separate conference from
april 29-30.
    Representatives of the Inter American Development Bank, the
International Monetary Fund and other international financial
organizations will attend both conferences.
    The Central Bank of Barbados gave no agenda for the
meetings
