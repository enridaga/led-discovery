Noland Co said its May sales of
35 mln dlrs was down 11 pct from the year-earlier period of
38.8 mln dlrs.
    For the first five months of 1987, the company said its
sales totaled 161.8 mln dlrs, four pct less than the first five
months of 1986 when the company posted sales of 169.0 mln dlrs.
