<p>Mad. de la Grange sung in Rossini&rsquo;s &ldquo;Barber,&rdquo; Bellini&rsquo;s &ldquo;Lucia,&rdquo; Meyerbeer&rsquo;s &ldquo;Robert&rdquo; and &ldquo;Prophet,&rdquo; and also Mozart&rsquo;s &ldquo;Magic Flute.&rdquo; It hardly need be added that she met with a warm reception, and, at the increased prices, drew a crowded house.</p>
