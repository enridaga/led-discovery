<pre>For Drury Lane, I composed an afterpiece, called "The Chimney Corner," translated <br />from the French by Mr. Walsh Porter, an excellent, though a very eccentric man; <br />to whom, as I have already said, I was indebted for many attentions when at <br />Worcester, and elsewhere. The scenery was very pretty, but the piece was not <br />successful; and after its third representation, was withdrawn.</pre>
