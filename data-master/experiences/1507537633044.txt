<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;">The twenty-third day of April was St. George's Day. And at Whitehall the Queen's Grace went from her chapel with a twelve knights of the Garter in robes with collars of gold, with Garters. And twenty of her chapel in copes of cloth of gold to the offering,&nbsp;singing the English procession from the chapel round about the hall and back again to the chapel singing.&nbsp;</span></p>
