<pre>Mrs. Billington was engaged this year as prima donna at the Opera House. She made <br />her first appearance in the serious opera of "Ferdinand in Mexico," by Nassolini, <br />a charming composer ; and in the month of May, Winter composed, expressly for her, <br />the opera of " Calypso," the music of which she sang delightfully, and looked the <br />character divinely. </pre>
