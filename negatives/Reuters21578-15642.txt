Emery Air Freight Corp, which
acquired controlling interest in Purolator Courier Corp &lt;PCC>,
said it has named John Kilcullen executive vice president and
chief operating officer of Purolator.
    Previously, Emery said Kilcullen was Purolator's executive
vice president of operations.
    Emery said Kilcullen now heads the Purolator executive
management team and reports to Emery President Denis McCarthy.
