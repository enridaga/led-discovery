Spendthrift Farm Inc said it has
consented to its removal from the American Stock Exchange due
to its failure to meet financial listing requirements.
    The last day for Spendthrift trading shall be June 26.
    The company also said its board named Dale McDoulett,
formerly president of Diamond Shamrock International Petroleum
Co, as president replacing Robert Hagopian who resigned
February 7.
