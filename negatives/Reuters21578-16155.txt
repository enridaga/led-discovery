Bromon Aircraft Co, which is privately
held, said it is in the final design phase of a twin turboprop,
and is scheduled to complete production of three prototype
aircraft in the fall of 1988.
    It also said it expects certification by the Federal
Aviation Administration in 1988.
    The turboprop, the BR-2000, can carry up to 46 passengers
or up to six tons of cargo, according to the company.
