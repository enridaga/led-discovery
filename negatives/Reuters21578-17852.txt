Conservative Party chairman Norman Tebbit,
who plans to give up the post an a date yet to be set, is
joining Blue Arrow Plc &lt;BAWL.L> as a non-executive director,
Blue Arrow said.

Employment agency &lt;Manpower Inc>, said Tebbit is joining the

November 1.
