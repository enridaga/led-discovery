Perry Drug Stores Inc said
February sales were up 19.5 pct to 52 mln dlrs from 43 mln dlrs
a year earlier, with same-store sales up five pct.
    It said sales for the first four months of its fiscal year
were up 17.0 pct to 241 mln dlrs from 206 mln dlrs a year
before, with same-store sales up five pct.
