Corvus Systems Inc said it has
rached an agreement to sell networking products for personal
computers to Bell Canada Enterprises Inc.
    It said Bell Canada will remarket the products as part of
its LANSCAPE line throughout Ontario and Quebec.
    Corvus said it expects the agreement to have a major impact
on its sales of networking products for years.
    It said products involved include its Omninet interface
boards for networking International Business Machines Corp
&lt;IBM> and compatible computers on Omninet networks, the HUB
controller, network operating software and cabling components.
