<p>So, indeed, were they all; and I shall&nbsp;never forget the rapture of attention and glow of excitement with which they listened to some sketchy souvenirs&nbsp;of Wagner, Berlioz, and Liszt, which I played to them&nbsp;on a dreadful old grand piano one day, when I had bidden them to my hotel for the entertainment of a few&nbsp;friends [in Burcharest]. It was a treat to observe their intense appreciation of harmonic methods and treatments absolutely new&nbsp;to them, and to listen to their intelligent attempts to fish out the novelties on their own instruments afterwards.</p>
