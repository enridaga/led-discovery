<pre>In my intercourse with theatrical ladies, I never met with more equanimity of <br />temper and good nature than in Mrs. Davison. Her mother was then with her, and <br />played the old ladies in comedy and opera extremely well, and bore the character <br />of an excellent parent, and kind-hearted woman. Mrs. Billington and I took our <br />benefits, and both had crowded houses. After finishing our engagements at <br />Liverpool, we went to Edinburgh.</pre>
