<p style="margin: 0px; font-size: 9.1px; font-family: Arial;">a) Hearing Tocanini conduct his last concert in Britain - the Brahms 3rd and 4th Symphonies.&nbsp; B)&nbsp; Sir Thomas Beecham's first concert at the Festival Hall playing Sibelius's second symphony.&nbsp; He had been rather sniffy about the hall but this did not stop him giving a brilliant performance.&nbsp; Thirty odd years later I was driving on the motorway and turned the radio on to find they were playing Sibelius's second.&nbsp; After about 5 minutes I came to the conclusion it was Beecham's Festival Hal performance I had attended.&nbsp; It was.</p>
