<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 12.55pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; margin: 0cm 56.0pt .0001pt 13.0pt;">Of Mrs. Bates I am much better able to speak, having frequently met her in private society, and heard her sing repeatedly. Her voice was perhaps not so <em style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal;">celestial</em> as Mrs. Sheridan&rsquo;s but was nevertheless one of the finest quality; full, rich, and commanding, of great compass, flexibility, and power.</p>
