<p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-pagination: none; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial; mso-ansi-language: EN-US;">When they came to the <em><span style="mso-bidi-font-style: italic;">Piazza di Scossa Cavalli</span> </em>(about a quarter of a Mile from the Church, they call it a great deal more) they fetch'd a Compass about that Piazza, and went up by the Portico on the other side the Area before S. <em><span style="mso-bidi-font-style: italic;">Peter</span></em>'s into the Church. His Holiness ascending the great Altar, gave his Benediction, and elevated the Host. At the Elevation, there was heard such a Sound of the People thumping their Breasts, as you hear when a Regiment of Soldiers are grounding their Musquets.&mdash;Anthems singing before and after. </span></p>
