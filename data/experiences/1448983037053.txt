<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 12.55pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; margin: 0cm 56.0pt .0001pt 0cm;">[&hellip;] the recitative, &ldquo;Ye sacred priests,&rdquo; and air, &ldquo;Farewell, ye limpid streams,&rdquo; performed by Miss Stephens, who was once more within her compass, and sung them with great sweetness; perhaps with not quite so much spirit as might be wished in the, last lively movement, but on the whole very well.</p>
