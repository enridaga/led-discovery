<p>From this we went at ten o&rsquo;clock to the &ldquo;Grosser Kirche,&rdquo; also Protestant. Here the house was larger, but the congregation smaller. The order of service was nearly the same; the organist acted also as precentor, or leader of the singing; besides him there was no choir; he, both by his instrument and his voice, caused himself to be heard.</p>
