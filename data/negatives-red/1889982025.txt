"On Monday morning Grahame came down to breakfast, read your St John Long;, and insisted on my riding up with him to Grange: we went by Waterbeck and Torbeckhill, over the wet moor; had a meek, gently pleasant afternoon; I returned about eight o'clock, and found &mdash; O wonder and terror &mdash; an Express from Dumfries with tidings that the Jeffreys had notified that they would all be at Craigenputtoch that night!'"
