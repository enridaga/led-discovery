"It does my heart good &mdash; I, who have been so deeply distressed by the tone of the newspapers since my return from France &mdash; to hear the soldiers reading them with running comment. """"If those newspaper blokes that go to the front would ask the fellows in the trenches straight, they'd hear a different story."""" Reading a picture paper account of Silvertown, where soldiers are depicted clearing away <i>debris</i> """"with great energy, but showing clearly their eagerness to be at grips with the enemy"""" &mdash; chorus: """"I don't think.""""'"
