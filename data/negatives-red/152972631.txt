"On Wednesdays the bells of St. Michael's Church on the neighbouring hill pealed for a service or, as some said, """"choir practice"""". They filled me with dread, a reminder of Sunday yet to come. In <i>Eric or Little by Little</i> which I had begun to read, the bell was always tolling. <i>Or the World of School</i> it said, and in that school it seemed that the boys died off like flies.'"
