Bell Canada Enterprises Inc said it
extended to June 30 from June 4 a short-term voluntary sale
program for shareholders owning 10 or fewer Bell Canada common
shares.
    The plan allows such shareholders of record at April 21 to
sell their shares at market price without incurring brokerage
commissions or other charges.
    Bell Canada was quoted at 42 dlrs, up 1/4, in afternoon
trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange.
