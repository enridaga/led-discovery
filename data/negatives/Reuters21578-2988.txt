Honeybee Inc said it knows of no reason
for the recent rise in its stock price.
    "There are no corporate or business developments, announced
or unannounced," the company said in a statement.  "Business is
continuing as usual," it said, adding that it is not engaged in
any merger or acquisition discussions.
    The company's stock is trading up 2-1/8 points at 14-1/4.
    Honeybee said it plans to release later this month
preliminary financial results for the year ended January 30.
