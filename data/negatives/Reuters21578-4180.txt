Paco Pharmaceutical Services Inc said
its common stock began trading today on the New York Stock
Exchange.
    The company's stock opened at 26-3/4 and closed at 26-1/8
on 21,900 shares traded.
      Paco said NASDAQ will continue to carry its common stock
warrants due to expire Decmber 31 under the symbol &lt;PPSIW> and
its convertible debentures issued February six under the symbol
&lt;PPSIG>.
