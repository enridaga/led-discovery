Mitsubishi Electric Corp &lt;MIET.T> said it
is nearing an agreement with Unisys Corp &lt;UIS> of the U.S. To
supply central processing units for a medium-sized Unisys
mainframe computer planned to succeed the System 80.
    A Mitsubishi Electric official declined to say how many
processors would be involved, but said there are no plans to
market the new Unisys computer in Japan.
