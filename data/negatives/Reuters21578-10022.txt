GenRad Inc said it introduced a
software product that allows for development of test programs
for its 227X line of in-circuit/functional test systems on
Digital Equipment Corp's &lt;DEC> VAX-based computers.
    GenRad said the product, named ATG-32, provides for
off-line development of test systems at four- to eight-times
the speed of its current 227X test program.
    It said ATG-32 is priced at 30,000 dlrs and is available
immediately.
