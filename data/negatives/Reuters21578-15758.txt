Gull Inc said it has received a
contract initially worth about 1,500,000 dslrs to supply
McDonnell Douglas Corp with the control system for an On-Board
Inert Gas Generating System to be used in the new C-17A
military transport cargo airport.
    It said under production options in the coutract, it could
supply controllers for up to 210 aircraft, raising the value of
the contract to over 25 mln dlrs.
