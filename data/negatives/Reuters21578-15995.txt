The coupon on the 50 mln dlr, five year,
equity warrant eurobond for Tokyo Rope Manufacturing Co Ltd has
been set at two pct compared with an indicated level of 2-1/8
pct, lead manager Nikko Securities Co (Europe) Ltd said.
    The exercise price was set at 508 yen per share,
representing a premium of 2.63 pct over today's closing price
of 495 yen.
    The foreign exchange rate was set at 146.20 yen to the
dollar.
