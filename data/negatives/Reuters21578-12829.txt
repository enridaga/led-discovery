Manhattan National Corp said it elected
Charles Hinckley chairman and chief executive officer of the
life insurance holding company.
    Hinckley succeeds Wilmot Wheeler Jr, who returns to his
former position as vice chairman, the company said.
    Hinckley will continue as president and chief executive
officer of Union Central Life Insurance Co, a Cincinnati-based
mutual insurer, which owns approximately 52.2 pct of Manhattan
National outstanding stock.
