Elsinore Corp said it deposited
5,795,833 dlrs with Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co  the trustee
of its Elsinore Finance Corp unit's 15-1/2 pct senior mortgage
bonds due 1999.
    The deposit represents the balance of accrued and unpaid
interest on the bonds thorugh June 30, 1986.
    Elsinore Corp has guaranteed payment on the bonds. Payment
of this amount, about 50.40 dlrs per 1,000 dlrs principal, will
be made on July 14 to shareholders of record July 13.
