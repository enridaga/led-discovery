Canada is issuing an 80 billion yen
eurobond due June 25, 1992 paying 4-3/8 pct and priced at
101-1/2 pct, lead manager Nomura International Ltd said.
    The non-callable bond is available in denominations of one
mln and 10 mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg. The
selling concession is 1-1/4 pct while management and
underwriting combined pays 5/8 pct.
    Payment date is June 25.
