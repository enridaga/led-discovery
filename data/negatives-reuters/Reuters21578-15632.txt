&lt;Alger Inc> said it will sell the Alger
Fund through broker-dealers as a result of its agreement with
Dealer Network Services, a division of the newly-established
&lt;Rutland Securities L.P.>.
    The fund formerly was sold only directly to investors,
Alger said.
    Dealer Network Services is organizing a countrywide group
of established broker-dealers to sell the Alger Fund, Alger
said.
