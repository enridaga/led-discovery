GTE Corp's GTE EFT Services Inc
unit and Indiana National Corp said they have signed an
agreement where GTE will provide electronic funds transfer
switching services for Indiana National's MoneyMover 24-Hour
Teller and Plus System networks.
    The companies said GTE EFT Service will make an initial
hookup with CommerceAmerica Banking Company, an affiliate of
Indiana National.
    The MoneyMover and Plus System networks support about 400
24-hour automated teller machines in more than 80 cities in
Indiana and Kentucky, the companies said.
