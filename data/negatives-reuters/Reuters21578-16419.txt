Toyota Motor Corp &lt;TOYO.T> is likely to
raise 800 mln dlrs through a Eurodollar bond with warrants in
mid-July, underwriting sources said.
    A final official decision on the issue has yet to be made
and detailed terms will be announced later, they added.
    The funds are likely to be used for investments to increase
productivity both domestically and abroad, they said.
