France's seasonally adjusted unemployment
rate rose to a record 11.1 pct of the workforce at end-March
compared with 11 pct at end-February, the National Statistics
Institute (INSEE) said.
    The total number of unemployed edged up to 2.67 mln at
end-March from 2.65 mln at end-February, an INSEE spokeswoman
said.
