Hog prices are
expected steady to 0.50 dlr higher on estimated receipts of
2,000 head, private sources said. Top seen at 51.00 to 51.50
dlrs per cwt.
    Sources said the market closed stronger late yesterday and
was expected to carry over into today's session.
    "Farmers are getting in the fields here," they said. Also,
little if any country movement was expected, they added.
