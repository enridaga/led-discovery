Chrysler Corp said it is extending its
low annual percentage rate (APR) financing, sales-incentive
program on some 1986 and 1987 model cars and trucks until April
10 from March 31.
    Chrysler said the program offers customers the choice
between Chrysler's low APR financing of 3.7 pct for 24 months,
5.9 pct for 36 months, 6.9 pct for 48 months or 9.9 pct for 60
months and a cash incentive, or a combination of low rate
financing through Chrysler Credit Corp plus a cash incentive.
