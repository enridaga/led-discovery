Toshiba Corp &lt;TSBA.T> said it has set up
a design centre for large integrated circuits (LSI) in Hong
Kong.
    The centre, located at its wholly-owned Hong Kong
subsidiary &lt;Toshiba Electronics Asia Ltd>, will start
operations to help create new demand for semi-customized LSI
from April 1.
    Toshiba has nine LSI design centres in the U.S., Europe and
Japan.
