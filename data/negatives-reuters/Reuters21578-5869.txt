Peking backed &lt;China Everbright
Holdings Co Ltd> said it signed an accord with a unit of U.S.
Based Lotus Development Inc &lt;LOTS.O> to form a joint venture

share in the 70 mln U.S. Dlr worth &lt;Everbright Lotus CRT
Manufacturing Ltd>, while &lt;H.K. Lotus Scientific Development
Ltd> will own the remaining stake.
    The plant has an annual production capacity of up to one
mln 14-26 inch cathode ray tubes for colour televisions.
