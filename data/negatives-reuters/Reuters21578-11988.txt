Chrysler Canada Ltd, wholly
owned by Chrysler Corp, said it sold 16,484 passenger cars in
March, a 9.7 pct increase over the 15,024 sold in the same
month of last year.
    The company said truck sales rose 32 pct to a March record
of 7,400 units from 5,603 in March of last year.
    For the first quarter, car sales totaled 34,578 compared
with 37,097 a year earlier, while truck sales totaled 15,397
compared with 14,069.
