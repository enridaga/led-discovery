Hyundai Motor America, a
unit of Hyundai Motor Co of South Korea, said domestic car
sales during the month of March rose to 19,502 compared witrh
10,432 during March last year.
    Year-to-date car sales rose to 56,164 from 11,020 during
the same period last year, the company said.
    Hyundai began selling cars in the United States last
February 20.
