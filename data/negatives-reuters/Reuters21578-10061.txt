The North Carolina Department
of Commerce said Electrolux AB of Sweden's White Consolidated
Industries subsidiary will locate a new 15 mln dlr 352,000
square foot national appliance parts distribution center at
Asheville, N.C.
    It said the center will handle parts for Frigidaire,
Tappan, Kelvinator, Gibson, Vesta and White-Westinghouse
product lines.  Each now has a separate distribution center.
    The department said construction will start immediately and
should be finished in late 1987.
