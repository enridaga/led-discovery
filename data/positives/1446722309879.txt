<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 12.4pt; mso-line-height-rule: exactly; margin: 0cm 23.0pt .0001pt 0cm;">When I first heard her [Mrs. Billington], in 1783, she was very young and pretty, had a delightful fresh voice of very high compass, and sung with great neatness several songs composed for Allegranti, whom she closely imitated.</p>
