<p>From that moment &mdash; although I only&nbsp;saw and heard him [Mendelssohn] once again, in the organ-loft of St.&nbsp;Paul's, when the rapidity of his pedal-playing well-nigh&nbsp;unsettled my reason &mdash; he became my idol and ideal, and&nbsp;remained so for years. It is certainly not too much to&nbsp;say that I worshipped him <em>dans mon for int&eacute;rieur</em> with&nbsp;all the ardour that was in me.</p>
