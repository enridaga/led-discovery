<p>On the last occasion on which I heard her [Sontag], she sung a duet with Pasta, which was most admirably performed, and their voices har&shy;monized better than could have been expected from the difference of their quality. These two great singers ought not to be put into comparison with each other; for while clear&shy;ness, neatness, and delicacy are the charac&shy;teristics of Sontag, power, energy, and strong expression are those of Pasta [&hellip;]</p>
