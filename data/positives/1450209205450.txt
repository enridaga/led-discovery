<p class="MsoNormal">Mr. Hatton&rsquo;s versatility was great. I remember an instance of it. One Saturday night in a Musical Fund concert, he played Mendelssohn&rsquo;s D-Minor Piano Concerto,&mdash;that being its first performance in Boston. Later, in the same concert, he sang some comic songs.&nbsp;</p>
